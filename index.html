What We Do

LAFONAT HOTEL focus on eco-friendly accommodations and amenities, such as sourcing ingredients locally, using green energy and products, and reducing the energy usage on property.

  • Green Energy

    We are using the solar panel system and harvesting the rain for energy-saving.

  • Local Ingredients

    We are strongly committed to using ingredients produced by organic farming.

  • Sustainable Products

    We are producing the organic linens and amenities, safe cleaning products.
